About
-----

Dr. Chandravesh Chaudhari is a researcher and practitioner working at the intersection of machine learning, business analytics, and product-focused data science. His work spans applied machine learning for industry use-cases, technical leadership in data-driven teams, and writing accessible resources that bridge theory and practice.

- Author of books and technical guides focused on applying machine learning in business contexts.
- Research and applied projects in predictive analytics, recommendation systems, and time-series forecasting.
- Experience designing data products, mentoring teams, and teaching workshops on ML for business audiences.
